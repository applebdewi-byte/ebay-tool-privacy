Privacy Policy for My eBay Automation Tool

Last Updated: [Wednesday's 03.09.2025]

By using this tool, you explicitly consent to the following:
  • You authorize eBay to share your account data with this application.
  • You grant this tool permission to act on your behalf on eBay to perform automated tasks.
  • You understand and agree to the data handling practices described below.

1. What Data We Access

This tool uses the eBay API to access the following types of data from your eBay account:

  • Account Information: Your eBay user ID, feedback score, and seller status.
  • Listing Data: Active, sold, and unsold listings including titles, descriptions, prices, and quantities.
  • Order Information: Order details, buyer information, shipping addresses, and order status.
  • Inventory Data: Your eBay inventory levels and product details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To automate listing creation and management on your behalf.
  • To synchronize and manage your eBay inventory levels.
  • To monitor and manage your eBay orders and sales performance.
  • To provide you with automated eBay selling services.

3. Data Storage and Security

We do not store your personal eBay data on any external servers.

  • All API calls are made directly between your device and eBay's servers.
  • Authentication tokens are stored locally on your device where the tool is running.
  • We do not create databases or store copies of your eBay data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute your eBay data to any third parties.

Your data remains between you, eBay, and this personal automation tool only.

5. Your Consent

By authenticating with eBay and using this tool, you consent to:

  • The collection and use of your eBay data as described in this policy.
  • The tool acting on your behalf to perform automated eBay tasks.
  • The data handling practices outlined here.

We may update this privacy policy periodically. The most current version will always be available at this URL.
